About Us

We are not your average IT crew.

We are a team of healthcare IT specialists who have spent 30 years earning the trust of the most demanding clinical environments in the country.

Our Story

Where we came from

IRG of Georgia was founded in 1994 with a simple mission — make healthcare IT installation easy. Over the next two decades we performed thousands of point-of-care installations across hundreds of facilities, working in every type of clinical environment from busy emergency departments to sensitive surgical suites.

In 2014 we rebranded as SeamlessPOC — a name that reflects exactly what our customers have always said about working with us. The word seamless kept coming up in testimonials, in phone calls, in thank-you notes. We made it official.

Today we are more than just an installation crew. We are a valued resource for everything point-of-care — from surgery tracking boards and anesthesia cart retrofits to cable management and full device refresh projects. We have become the team that hospital IT directors call first.
