About Us

We are not your average IT crew.

We are a team of healthcare IT specialists who have spent 30 years earning the trust of the most demanding clinical environments in the country.

Our Journey

Built Over Three Decades

1994

Founded as IRG of Georgia with a straightforward mission: provide healthcare facilities with reliable, expert IT installation services that their internal teams couldn't handle alone.

2000s

Grew alongside the healthcare IT industry — installing thousands of workstations, configuring hundreds of departments, and developing deep expertise in the unique challenges of clinical environments.

2014

Rebranded as SeamlessPOC to reflect our evolved focus on point-of-care technology and our commitment to making every aspect of the installation process truly seamless.

Today

30+ years and still the team hospitals call first. Thousands of installations across hundreds of facilities. The same values, the same commitment, the same results.
