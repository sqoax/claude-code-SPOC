Our Story

Built on Experience, Driven by Purpose

In 1994, we founded IRG with a straightforward mission: provide healthcare facilities with reliable, expert IT installation services. At the time, hospitals were just beginning to digitize their workflows, and most didn't have the internal resources to manage the physical infrastructure that came with it.

Over the next two decades, we grew alongside the healthcare IT industry — installing thousands of workstations, configuring hundreds of departments, and developing deep expertise in the unique challenges of clinical environments. We learned that healthcare IT isn't just about technology. It's about understanding how nurses, physicians, and administrators work, and making sure the technology serves them — not the other way around.

In 2014, we rebranded as SeamlessPOC to reflect our evolved focus on point-of-care technology and our commitment to making every aspect of the installation process truly seamless. The name changed, but the values stayed the same: do exceptional work, communicate transparently, and treat every facility like it's our only client.
