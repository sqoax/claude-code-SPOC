Project Types

The Work We Do Every Day

Bedside EMR

Integrating in-room EMR devices into clinical workflow without diminishing patient interaction or clinician efficiency. We mount, cable, and configure the physical workspace so the technology works with your team — not against them.

Labor and Delivery Carts

Specialized cart deployment in L&D environments where reliability and cleanliness are non-negotiable. We extend the ROI of these assets and enhance the workstation experience for nursing staff.

Surgery Tracking Boards

The key to successful surgery tracking boards is visibility and accessibility. We handle the full installation — mounting, cabling, and positioning — so every stakeholder can see what they need to see.

Anesthesia Carts

Installing a PC on an anesthesia machine can seem impossible. SeamlessPOC has done it hundreds of times. We work directly with your anesthesia and IT teams to find the right mounting solution for each machine configuration.

Digital Signage

Make vital information available to direct and inform guests, visitors, and clinical staff. We install and cable digital signage displays throughout your facility with minimal disruption to daily operations.

Conference Rooms

From presentation displays to full video conferencing setups, we mount and cable conference room technology to give maximum results using minimum space.

Infection Control Solutions

Reducing hospital acquired infections starts with a cleaner physical environment. We install solutions designed to improve cleanability, reduce surface contact points, and support your infection control protocols.

Cable Management and Cleanup

Before and after photos say it all. We take cluttered unsafe cable environments and turn them into clean professional workspaces. Custom cable lengths, bundled runs, and stowed hardware — done right the first time.

Creating Workspace

When new technologies are incorporated at the point of care, workspace has to be created out of thin air. We specialize in finding mounting and storage solutions that maximize limited clinical space without compromising workflow.

The Seamless Desktop

A more visually appealing admissions area and an organized workspace for staff. We transform cluttered front-desk environments into clean professional workstations that make a strong first impression on patients.
