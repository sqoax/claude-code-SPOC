Solutions

Real projects. Real results.

From single-room upgrades to 400-unit hospital rollouts — here's the kind of work we do every day.

Bedside EMR

Wall-mounted and arm-based workstations that bring the electronic medical record to the patient bedside. We handle mount selection, installation, cable routing, and ergonomic positioning for every room type.

Labor & Delivery Carts

Mobile workstations configured for the fast pace and unique requirements of L&D units. Custom setups with fetal monitoring integration, med-safe power, and easy-clean surfaces.

Surgery Tracking Boards

Large-format displays and mounting systems for real-time surgical case tracking and family communication. Professional installation with concealed cabling and network connectivity.

Anesthesia Carts

Ruggedized workstation setups optimized for the demanding anesthesia workflow in operating suites. Space-efficient designs built for the tight quarters of an OR environment.

Digital Signage

Wayfinding displays, patient education screens, and facility communication boards — installed, configured, and managed. From lobby directories to department-specific dashboards.

Conference Rooms

Integrated AV, display, and connectivity solutions for clinical conference rooms and telemedicine suites. Clean wall mounts, hidden cables, and reliable connections every time.

Infection Control Solutions

Hardware selection and installation techniques that support your facility's infection prevention standards. Sealed cable paths, antimicrobial-compatible mounts, and easy-clean configurations.

Cable Management & Cleanup

Comprehensive re-cabling for existing installations. We untangle, organize, label, and secure every cable run — transforming chaotic setups into clean, maintainable infrastructure.

Creating Workspace

Building new IT workspaces within existing clinical areas. From charting alcoves to nursing station upgrades, we design and build functional work environments where they didn't exist before.

The Seamless Desktop

Our signature installation standard — monitor arms, keyboard trays, cable management, and power all integrated into one clean, ergonomic workstation. The gold standard for point-of-care computing.
