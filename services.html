Cable Management

Messy cables are not just an eyesore. They are a safety issue.

Tangled cords on the floor are a trip hazard. Hardware sitting on desks reduces workspace. Equipment that cannot breathe runs hot and fails early. SeamlessPOC cable management is a proven method for improving workplace safety, cleanability, and the professional appearance of every clinical space. We stow hardware off the floor, cut custom cable lengths so nothing is dragging, and bundle everything neatly in less visible locations. Your clinical staff will notice the difference on day one.

  • ✓ Hardware stowing and mounting
  • ✓ Custom cable length replacement
  • ✓ Cable bundling and securing
  • ✓ Floor clearance for full mop access
  • ✓ CPU ventilation and airflow optimization
