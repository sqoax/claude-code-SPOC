Our Process

No guesswork. No surprises. No callbacks.

Every SeamlessPOC project follows the same proven process — because your facility deserves consistency not improvisation.

Step by Step

How Every Project Unfolds

Five phases. One accountable team. Zero surprises.

1

Workflow Analysis

We start before anything is ordered or scheduled. A SeamlessPOC representative meets with your team onsite to understand the clinical workflow, the physical environment, and the specific constraints of each department. We ask the right questions so nothing gets missed — and so you never have to repeat yourself once the project starts.

2

Product Evaluation

There are hundreds of choices for articulating mounts, mobile carts, wall cabinets, and point-of-care components. We narrow it down for you. We install evaluation units so your clinical staff can actually use the products and give real input before a purchase decision is made. No guessing. No buyer's remorse.

3

Procurement

Once the right solution is identified we handle everything. One quote covers product from multiple vendors, installation materials, labor, and warranty coverage. You issue one purchase order. We coordinate the rest. No chasing down five different vendors for five different invoices.

4

Installation

Our factory-trained fully credentialed technicians execute the install cleanly and professionally. We work in live clinical environments every day — we understand patient privacy, infection control, and how to operate without disrupting care. Evening and weekend scheduling is available. When we finish the space is cleaner than when we arrived.

5

Post-Install Support

We do not install and disappear. SeamlessPOC offers maintenance visits, asset protection plans, and lifecycle management to keep your equipment performing long after go-live. If something needs attention we show up with the same team that did the original install.
